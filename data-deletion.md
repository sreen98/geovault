# GeoVault — Account & Data Deletion

**App name:** GeoVault  
**Developer:** developersreenz@gmail.com

## How to Delete Your Data

GeoVault stores all data locally on your device. There is no server-side account or database. You have full control over your data at all times.

### Delete Individual Places
1. Open GeoVault
2. Tap on any saved place
3. Tap the delete button
4. Confirm deletion

### Delete All Local Data
1. Open GeoVault
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Scroll to **Data Management**
4. Tap **Clear All Data**
5. Confirm to permanently delete all saved places

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all local data from your device.

### Delete Google Drive Backup Data
If you enabled Google Drive backup, a copy of your data is stored in a hidden app-specific folder on your Google Drive. To remove it:

1. Go to [Google Account Settings](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
2. Find **GeoVault** in the list of third-party apps
3. Click **Remove Access**

This will revoke GeoVault's access and delete the hidden app data folder containing your backup.

### What Gets Deleted

| Data type | Where stored | How to delete |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| Saved places (names, coordinates, notes, tags) | Device only | Clear All Data or uninstall |
| Google email (for backup display) | Device only | Sign out of Google in Settings or uninstall |
| Backup file | Your Google Drive (hidden folder) | Remove Access from Google Account Settings |

### Data We Do NOT Store

- No server-side accounts
- No analytics data
- No advertising profiles
- No data on our servers (we do not operate any servers)
